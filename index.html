Angela,s Recipe for the Best
Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 3/4 teaspoon yeast
  • 1/4 cup granulated salt
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

For the Filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2tablesppoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast ,sugar,eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. Transfer the dough into large bowl
